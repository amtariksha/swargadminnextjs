# Privacy Notice

**Version v1.0 · effective from publication date**

Swarg Food (operated by Amtariksha Tech Pvt Ltd, Bangalore) respects your
privacy. This notice explains, in plain language, what personal data we
collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and what choices you have.

This notice is published under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act,
2023 (DPDP Act) and the Digital Personal Data Protection Rules, 2025.

## 1. Who we are

- **Data Fiduciary**: Amtariksha Tech Pvt Ltd, Bangalore, India.
- **Contact for privacy questions**: privacy@swargfood.com
- **Grievance redressal officer**: Pradeep (founder).

## 2. What data we collect

We collect only what we need to serve you well:

- **Identity**: your name, phone number, email address.
- **Delivery**: your address, pincode, preferred delivery time.
- **Orders**: what you order, when, how often, payment method.
- **Communication**: messages you send us, calls you make, feedback you leave.
- **App usage**: app pages you visit, features you use. We do not track you
  across other apps or websites.

We do **not** collect health data, biometric data, location data outside the
delivery address, or financial data beyond what is needed to process payment.

## 3. Why we collect it

| Purpose | What we use it for |
|---|---|
| **Order fulfilment** | Process and deliver your orders. Send delivery updates. |
| **Customer support** | Reply to your questions, fix problems, handle refunds. |
| **Subscription management** | Run your milk / ghee / curd / paneer subscriptions, pause/resume, billing. |
| **Marketing** *(consent required)* | Tell you about new products, festival pre-orders, replenishment reminders, special offers. You can opt out anytime. |
| **Analytics** *(consent required)* | Understand how customers use the app so we can improve it. We use aggregated data, never your individual behaviour, for this. |

## 4. Your consent

For **order fulfilment, delivery, customer support, and subscriptions** — we
process your data on the legitimate basis of our contract with you. No
separate consent toggle is needed for these.

For **marketing communication** (WhatsApp, email, SMS, in-app messages,
push notifications) — we process your data **only with your explicit consent**.
You give consent per channel and per purpose, and you can withdraw it at any time.

For **personalised recommendations and analytics** — same: opt-in, opt-out
controlled by you, recorded in our ledger.

## 5. How to control your consent

- **In the app**: Profile → Notifications → Preference Center. Toggle anything on or off.
- **On WhatsApp**: reply `STOP` to any marketing message to instantly opt out of WhatsApp marketing.
- **By email**: every marketing email has an unsubscribe link.
- **By phone or email**: contact privacy@swargfood.com.

Withdrawing consent stops future marketing immediately. It does not affect
the lawfulness of processing already done with your consent.

## 6. Your rights as a Data Principal

Under the DPDP Act, you have the right to:

1. **Access** your personal data.
2. **Correct** information that is inaccurate or out of date.
3. **Erase** your data (where we are not legally required to keep it — e.g.
   tax invoices must be kept for 7 years under GST law).
4. **Receive a copy** in a portable format.
5. **Withdraw consent** as described above.
6. **Nominate** another person to exercise these rights on your behalf in
   case of death or incapacity.
7. **Complain** to us first; if not resolved within 30 days, escalate to the
   Data Protection Board of India.

To exercise any right: email privacy@swargfood.com with your registered phone
number. We will verify your identity (an OTP to your registered number) and
respond within 7 calendar days as required by the DPDP Rules.

## 7. Who we share your data with

We share data only with vendors that help us deliver your order or
communicate with you. Every vendor is bound by a Data Processing Agreement.

- **Payment processor**: Razorpay (for payments).
- **WhatsApp Business**: Meta Platforms (for WhatsApp messaging).
- **SMS/email providers**: as needed for transactional notifications.
- **Cloud hosting**: Supabase, Vercel (for app infrastructure).
- **Delivery partners**: only the address and contact you provide for that delivery.

We do **not** sell your data. We do **not** share it with advertisers or data brokers.

## 8. How long we keep your data

- **Active customer data**: as long as you have an account.
- **Order and invoice data**: 7 years (required by GST law).
- **Consent records**: 7 years after the consent record was created (required by DPDP Act).
- **Marketing message logs**: 24 months for compliance auditing.
- **Inactive accounts** (no activity for 36 months): contacted with a renewal
  request, then anonymised or deleted as you choose.

## 9. How we keep your data safe

- Encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.
- Strict access controls — only employees who need data to do their job can see it.
- Vendor security reviews and Data Processing Agreements.
- Annual review of our security practices.

## 10. Data breaches

If a personal data breach occurs and affects you, we will notify you and the
Data Protection Board of India within 72 hours, per DPDP Rules.

## 11. Children

Our products are for adults. If you are under 18, please use Swarg with the
involvement of a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly profile minors for
marketing.

## 12. Changes to this notice

When we update this notice, we publish a new version (v1.1, v1.2, etc.) and
ask you to review and confirm your preferences. We never assume your
agreement; we always ask.
